# 🪝 Hooks Library

High-performing first lines. Use these to open threads, reels, TikToks, carousels.
A bad hook = invisible content. A great hook = 10x reach.

---

## The Rules of a Great Hook

1. **Make a bold claim** — challenge a belief
2. **Use a specific number** — "5 signs" not "some signs"
3. **Create curiosity gap** — hint at the answer without giving it
4. **Speak directly to the ICP** — "gym owners" not "businesses"
5. **First 3 seconds decide everything** on video

## Hooks to NEVER Use

❌ "Excited to announce..."
❌ "We're proud to share..."
❌ "Introducing our new..."
❌ "At Fiitsio, we believe..."
❌ "As a gym management platform..."

These are brand-speak. Nobody cares. Lead with the problem or the insight.
