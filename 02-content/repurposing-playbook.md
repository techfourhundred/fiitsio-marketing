# ♻️ Content Repurposing Playbook

## The Principle

One idea → 12 pieces of content → across 4 channels → for 4 weeks of posting.

Never create from scratch. Always extract maximum value from one insight.

---

## The Core Loop

```
INSIGHT (from a customer, data, or experience)
        ↓
  Long-Form Thread (X) — the foundation
        ↓
  ┌─────────────────────────────────────┐
  │  Carousel (Instagram/LinkedIn)       │
  │  Short video (TikTok/Reels)          │
  │  YouTube short                       │
  │  3x standalone tweets                │
  │  Reddit post (r/gymowner)            │
  │  Blog post (SEO)                     │
  │  Email newsletter                    │
  │  Instagram Story poll                │
  │  TikTok comment reply content        │
  │  LinkedIn post (Kevin's personal)    │
  └─────────────────────────────────────┘
```

---

## Example: "5 Signs a Member Will Cancel"

### Step 1: Write the thread (X)
```
THREAD: 5 signs a gym member is about to cancel — and what to do about each one 🧵

1/ They booked 5x/week in month 1. Now they haven't been in 3 weeks.
This is the #1 predictor of cancellation. Silence = danger.

Action: Automated WhatsApp message at day 14 of inactivity.
...
```

### Step 2: Turn into Instagram carousel
- Slide 1: Hook image — "5 signs your member is about to cancel"
- Slides 2–6: One sign per slide, bold visual
- Slide 7: CTA — "Want alerts when this happens? Link in bio"

### Step 3: Record TikTok/Reel (60–90s)
- Talk to camera, share all 5 signs conversationally
- Show Fiitsio dashboard with the retention alert feature at the end
- Caption: The signs + #gymowner #gymbusiness #fitnessapp

### Step 4: Extract 5 standalone tweets
Each sign becomes its own tweet with context + Fiitsio angle

### Step 5: YouTube Short
- Same TikTok video, slightly longer (60–90s), optimized title
- "5 Signs Your Gym Member Is About to Quit (And How to Stop Them)"

### Step 6: Blog post
- 1,500 words targeting: "gym member retention tips"
- Include Fiitsio screenshot showing the retention dashboard
- SEO: "how to reduce gym member cancellations"

### Step 7: Reddit/Community post
- r/gymowner post: "Here's what I learned about predicting gym member cancellations"
- No direct promotion — just share the insight, mention Fiitsio if asked

### Step 8: Email newsletter
- Subject: "Is member #47 about to quit your gym?"
- Use the same 5 signs, add a CTA to book a Fiitsio demo

## The 30-Minute Content Day

Kevin should be able to execute this in 30 min/day:

- **Monday (15 min):** Write the week's core thread on X
- **Tuesday (10 min):** Turn thread into carousel on Canva/Stories
- **Wednesday (30 min):** Record TikTok/Reel in the gym
- **Thursday (5 min):** Schedule the remaining tweets for the week
- **Friday (10 min):** Record a 60s YouTube Short from the week's thread

**Total: ~70 min/week. 12+ pieces of content.**
