# 📚 Content Pillars

## The 5 Pillars

Every piece of content maps to one of these pillars. Never post without knowing which pillar it serves.

---

### Pillar 1 — EDUCATION (40%)
*"Teach gym owners to run better businesses"*

**Formats:** Threads, carousels, short-form videos
**Goal:** Build authority, earn trust, attract SEO traffic

**Topics:**
- How to calculate your gym's churn rate
- 5 signs a member is about to cancel (and what to do)
- Why most gyms fail in year 3 (and how to avoid it)
- How to use WhatsApp automation without being spammy
- Pricing your memberships in 2025 — what data says
- How to run a class schedule that maximizes revenue
- The math behind member lifetime value (LTV)
- How to convert trial members into paying members
- Retention tactics that don't require discounts

---

### Pillar 2 — COMPETITOR ATTACKS (20%)
*"Be the category challenger — make competitors look bad"*

**Formats:** Short videos, comparison posts, threads
**Goal:** Capture people actively searching for alternatives

**Topics:**
- "Why I left Mindbody after 3 years" (told from owner's POV)
- Mindbody vs Fiitsio: honest comparison (cost breakdown)
- What Mindbody charges you that you don't realize
- "I switched from Glofox — here's what happened"
- Why do gym owners hate their software? (pain point content)
- The hidden fees every gym owner needs to know about

**Tone:** Bold, data-driven, never petty. Let the numbers do the talking.

---

### Pillar 3 — SOCIAL PROOF (20%)
*"Show, don't tell — let customers sell for you"*

**Formats:** Case studies, testimonials, before/after metrics
**Goal:** Build trust, handle objections, create FOMO

**Content:**
- "FitLab went from 12% monthly churn to 4% in 60 days"
- Screen recordings of gyms using Fiitsio live
- Member app screenshots (member booking, streak tracking)
- Owner dashboard walkthroughs ("look what you can see now")
- WhatsApp automation examples (real messages sent)

---

### Pillar 4 — FOUNDER STORY (10%)
*"Kevin building in public — the journey matters"*

**Formats:** Personal posts, behind-the-scenes, milestones
**Goal:** Build personal brand, create parasocial loyalty

**Topics:**
- Why I'm building Fiitsio (the origin story)
- Month 1 update: X gyms, Y MRR, what I learned
- The mistake that almost killed the launch
- Building a SaaS in Portugal — what's different
- What gym owners taught me about their business
- My honest take on [competitor news/moves]

---

### Pillar 5 — PRODUCT (10%)
*"Show the product — make people want it"*

**Formats:** Demo videos, feature spotlights, changelog posts
**Goal:** Convert warm audience into trials

**Topics:**
- "Watch a gym go from sign-up to first booking in 10 minutes"
- Feature spotlight: WhatsApp retention automation
- Feature spotlight: QR check-in + streak tracking
- New feature: [whatever shipped this week]
- "Here's what the member app looks like" (UX walkthrough)
