# 🎨 Brand Kit

## Brand Promise

> "The gym management platform gym owners actually love."

## Brand Personality

| Trait | What it means in practice |
|-------|--------------------------|
| **Direct** | Say what others won't. No fluff. |
| **Bold** | Challenge the status quo. Name competitors. Show the numbers. |
| **Warm** | We're on the gym owner's side. Not corporate. Human. |
| **Credible** | Every claim backed by data or proof. |
| **Optimistic** | This is fixable. Here's how. |

---

## Tone of Voice Examples

### Bold (use often):
> "Mindbody charges €400/month. We charge €79. Same features. No hidden fees."

### Direct:
> "Your member stopped booking 3 weeks ago. Do you know? Fiitsio does."

### Warm:
> "Running a gym is hard enough. Your software shouldn't make it harder."

### Credible:
> "Gyms using Fiitsio's retention alerts see 40% fewer cancellations in 90 days."

## Vocabulary Guide

**Use:**
- "Gym OS" (not "gym management software")
- "Gym owners" (not "fitness business operators")
- "Members" (not "clients" or "customers")
- "Retention" (not "churn reduction")
- "Alerts" (not "notifications")
- "Dashboard" (not "backend" or "portal")

**Avoid:**
- "Robust" / "enterprise-grade" / "comprehensive"
- "Solutions" / "leverage" / "synergy"
- "Seamlessly" / "effortlessly"
- "We're excited to announce"
- "Best-in-class"

## Visual Identity

### Colors
- **Primary:** Deep navy `#0A0F1E` + electric blue `#4F9EFF`
- **Accent:** Vibrant green `#00E5A0` (for success states, streaks, achievements)
- **Warning:** Amber `#FFB400` (for retention alerts)
- **Danger:** Red `#FF3B30` (for churn signals)
- **Background:** Off-white `#F8F9FC`

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Inter Bold / Clash Display
- **Body:** Inter Regular
- **Numbers/Data:** Inter Mono

### Photography Style
- Real gym environments (not stock photos)
- Real people (not models)
- Action shots (people training, not posing)
- App screenshots on real phones in real hands

### Social Media Templates
- Carousel: Dark background, bold white headline, brand green accent
- Quote cards: Gym owner face + quote + "Gym owner @ [gym name]"
- Data cards: Bold number, minimal text, navy + green

---

## Content Formats

### Thread template (X)
- Always lead with the hook tweet
- Number every insight
- End with Fiitsio soft CTA
- No more than 10 tweets

### Carousel template (Instagram)
- Slide 1: Bold hook text + arresting visual
- Slides 2–7: One insight per slide (max 30 words)
- Last slide: CTA + follow + Fiitsio
- Brand colors always consistent

### Video template
- First 3 seconds: Hook (no intro, no "hey guys")
- Middle: Value (fast-paced, no fluff)
- Last 5 seconds: CTA
- Captions always on

---

## Logo Usage

- Dark backgrounds: White logo
- Light backgrounds: Dark logo
- Never stretch, never use over busy photos without overlay
- Minimum size: 120px wide digital / 1 inch print
