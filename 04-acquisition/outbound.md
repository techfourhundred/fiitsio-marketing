# 📬 Outbound Playbook

## The Principle

For the first 10 gyms: Kevin does outbound directly.
No SDR. No agency. Founder-led sales is the most effective at this stage.

**Rule:** Never automate outreach until you have 5 conversations/week happening naturally.

---

## Who to Contact

### Tier 1 — Disgruntled Mindbody/Glofox customers
These are WARM leads. They're already in pain.

**Where to find them:**
- r/gymowner: Search "leaving Mindbody", "Mindbody alternative", "switching from Glofox"
- Google Maps: Search "gym" in Lisbon/Porto, check reviews for software complaints
- Facebook groups: "Gym Owners Portugal", "Fitness Business Network"
- Trustpilot: Glofox/Mindbody negative reviews — find the gym owner's name

**Outreach approach:**
```
Subject: Saw your Mindbody review — quick question

Hi [Name],

Saw your review on [Trustpilot/Reddit] about [specific pain point they mentioned].

I'm building Fiitsio — a gym management platform specifically designed to solve exactly that.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call? I'll show you how we handle [their specific complaint].

No pitch. Just want to understand your situation.

Kevin
Founder, Fiitsio
```

### Tier 2 — Cold outreach to gym owners

**Target gyms:**
- 50–500 members (too small for enterprise, too big for spreadsheets)
- Has a website/booking system (proves they care about tech)
- Located in Portugal (Phase 1)

**Finding them:**
- Google Maps: "ginásio [city]", "crossfit [city]", "estúdio fitness [city]"
- Instagram: Search #ginasiolisboa, #boxcrossfit, #estudiodefitness
- LinkedIn: "Gym owner" + Portugal

**Outreach sequence:**

**Email 1 (Day 1):**
```
Subject: Quick question about [Gym Name]'s booking system

Hi [Name],

Found [Gym Name] on Google — great reviews!

I'm building Fiitsio, a new gym management platform for Portuguese gyms. 
We support MB Way and Multibanco natively (something Mindbody and Glofox still can't do properly).

Would you be open to a 15-min call to show you what we're building?

I'm not trying to sell you on day 1 — I'm looking for gym owners to give honest feedback in exchange for a very favorable early adopter rate.

Kevin
```

**If no reply — Follow-up (Day 5):**
```
Hi [Name] — following up on my last message.

I know you're busy running [Gym Name]. I'll keep this short:

We're offering the first 10 Portuguese gyms a permanent 50% discount in exchange for honest feedback as we build.

Worth 15 minutes?

Kevin
```

**If no reply — Final (Day 10):**
```
Last follow-up, I promise.

Just wanted to leave you with this: the average Portuguese gym paying Mindbody spends €4,800/year on software. We charge €79/month.

If that's interesting to revisit when the timing is right, I'm here.

Kevin
fiitsio.com
```

## Sales Call Framework

### Discovery Questions (first call — 15 min)
1. How many active members do you have right now?
2. What software are you using today? What do you love/hate about it?
3. What's your biggest operational headache right now?
4. How much are you spending on software/month?
5. What would you do with 5 extra hours a week?

### Demo Flow (call 2 — 20 min)
1. Show the dashboard from an owner's POV
2. Show the member app UX
3. Show retention analytics (the "wow" moment)
4. Show WhatsApp automation
5. Show MB Way / Multibanco checkout
6. CTA: "Want to start a free trial?"

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "I'm happy with Mindbody" | "What would it take for you to leave? Most owners say [X]. Does that apply to you?" |
| "Too busy to switch" | "We migrate your data in 48 hours. You can run both in parallel for 2 weeks." |
| "Too expensive" | "What are you paying now? [pause] So we'd save you €[X]/month. Switching pays for itself in week 1." |
| "We need to think about it" | "What's the one question you need answered to decide? Let me answer it now." |
| "Not the right time" | "When IS the right time? Let's put a date in the calendar." |
