# ▶️ YouTube Playbook

## YouTube: Long Game, High ROI

YouTube is not an immediate win. But **a gym owner who watches a 10-minute Fiitsio video is 10x more likely to book a demo** than one who sees a tweet.

YouTube = trust at scale.

**Priority:** YouTube Shorts first (6–12 months) → Long form when you have 3+ customers willing to appear on camera.

---

## YouTube Shorts (Phase 1 — Now)

Repurpose TikToks and Reels directly to YouTube Shorts.
Same video, slightly different title optimization.

**Advantage:** YouTube Shorts feed is less competitive than TikTok.
One good short can drive YouTube subscribers who then watch long-form later.

**Process:**
1. Record TikTok
2. Upload same video to YouTube Shorts
3. Optimize title for search: "How to reduce gym churn" not just "Gym owner tips"
4. Add to "Shorts" playlist

## Long-Form Strategy (Phase 2 — Month 3+)

### Content Pillar: "The Gym OS Series"

A documentary-style series following real gym owners using Fiitsio.

**Episode structure:**
```
- Ep 1: "[Gym Name] — From Chaos to Clarity" (origin, problems, Fiitsio solution)
- Ep 2: "30 Days on Fiitsio — What Changed?"
- Ep 3: "The €400/Month Mindbody Migration" (switching story)
- Ep 4: "Building the Gym That Runs Itself"
```

### Content Pillar: "Gym Business Masterclass"

10–20 minute educational videos targeting gym owners.

**SEO-driven titles:**
- "How to Calculate Your Gym's Member Churn Rate (Step by Step)"
- "Gym Pricing Strategy 2025 — What Actually Works"
- "How to Use WhatsApp to Reduce Gym Member Cancellations"
- "Gym Management Software Comparison — Honest Review"
- "Mindbody Alternative — What I Switched to and Why"

### Content Pillar: "Build in Public with Kevin"

Monthly founder update videos.
- "Month X Building Fiitsio — X Gyms, €Y MRR, Lessons Learned"
- Transparent, raw, build trust
- Appeals to entrepreneur audience who might share/subscribe

## Video Production (Budget: Low)

**Setup:**
- iPhone 15 / good mirrorless camera
- Ring light or window light
- Lav mic or Blue Yeti
- iMovie / CapCut for editing (fast)

**No studio needed.** Authenticity > production quality for this audience.

**Template:** 
1. Hook (first 15 seconds — state the promise)
2. Why it matters (30 seconds)
3. The content (bulk of video)
4. Fiitsio CTA (last 60 seconds)
5. "Subscribe if you want more like this"

## The Compounding Effect

YouTube has the best long-term ROI of any content channel:
- Videos rank in Google Search
- Videos live forever (unlike tweets and reels)
- Subscribers compound over time
- One 100K-view video can drive 50+ demo requests

**Plant seeds now. Harvest in 12 months.**
