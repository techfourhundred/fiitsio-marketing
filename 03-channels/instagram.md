## Reels Strategy

### Perform best:
- Problem → Solution in 30–60 seconds
- Before/after transformations (gym metrics, not bodies!)
- "Did you know" fast-cut style
- Founder POV / behind the scenes
- App demo with satisfying UX moments

### Reel Formats to Test

**Format 1: Metric Reveal**
- Start with problem stat on screen
- Cut to dashboard showing the solution in Fiitsio
- End with CTA

**Format 2: Gym Owner Walkthrough**
- Film inside a real gym using the app
- "Here's how [Gym Name] tracks member retention"
- Show the dashboard, QR check-in, WhatsApp automation

**Format 3: "Things no one tells you about running a gym"**
- 5 fast cuts, each with a different insight
- Last insight: Fiitsio solves all of these

**Format 4: Competitor comparison**
- Side by side: Mindbody UI vs Fiitsio UI
- Price comparison
- "Same features. 1/5 the price."

---

## Carousel Strategy

Carousels get saved and shared more than any other format.
Each carousel should be **worth saving**.

### Carousel formats:

**"5 things gym owners wish they knew before opening"**
- Slide 1: Hook (bold, visual)
- Slides 2-6: One thing per slide (icon + headline + 2-3 lines)
- Slide 7: The Fiitsio angle
- Slide 8: CTA (save this, share it, DM us)

**"The math behind your gym's churn problem"**
- Slide 1: "Your gym loses how much to churn?"
- Slide 2: Formula (members × LTV × churn rate)
- Slides 3-6: How to calculate it for your gym
- Slide 7: "Fiitsio calculates this automatically"

**"Mindbody vs Fiitsio: What €400/month actually gets you"**
- Comparison slide by slide — feature by feature
- Price breakdown
- Hidden fees exposed

## Stories Strategy

**Daily Stories cadence:**
- Morning: Engagement prompt (poll, question, quiz)
- Midday: Behind the scenes / in progress
- Evening: Value-add (tip, insight, or quick win)

**Story ideas:**
- "Ask me anything about running a gym" (Q&A sticker)
- Poll: "What's your biggest pain as a gym owner?" (get ICP data)
- "Swipe up to book a free demo" (with demo video preview)
- Behind the scenes of building Fiitsio
- Gym owner shoutout / customer of the week
- Product update announcement
