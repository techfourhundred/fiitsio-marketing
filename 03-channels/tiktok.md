## Content Formats That Win

### 1. "Day in the life" — a gym running on Fiitsio
Walk through a real gym day using the app.
- Owner books class from dashboard
- Member checks in with QR code
- Retention alert fires — owner sends WhatsApp
- End: "This used to take me 2 hours. Now it's 10 minutes."

### 2. "I switched from [X]" testimonial format
Get a gym owner on camera:
- "I was paying €400/month for Mindbody. Here's what switching to Fiitsio did to my revenue."
- No script needed. Raw = authentic = performs better.

### 3. Reaction content
- React to gym owners complaining about software on TikTok
- React to Mindbody/Glofox TikToks
- React to common gym management advice with the Fiitsio angle

### 4. "Did you know" format
Fast-cut, text overlay, gym background:
- "Did you know most gyms lose 30% of members in the first 90 days?"
- Next cut: "And never know why?"
- Next cut: "Fiitsio tells you exactly which members are at risk."
- CTA: "Free trial link in bio"

### 5. Tutorial/How-to
- "How to set up WhatsApp retention automation in under 5 minutes"
- "How to see which of your members are about to quit"
- "How I booked 50 new members in January without spending on ads"

## Video Production Rules

**Length:** 30–90 seconds (sweet spot: 45–60s)
**Ratio:** Vertical, 9:16 always
**Captions:** Always on (80% watch without sound)
**Hook:** First 1–2 seconds must grab. No intros.

**Equipment:**
- iPhone in a good light — that's it
- Ring light if filming indoors at night
- Lavalier mic for cleaner audio (cheap on Amazon)

**Posting:**
- 3–5 videos/week minimum for growth
- Post when your audience is active: 7–9am, 12–2pm, 7–10pm
- Engage with comments for first 30 minutes after posting
