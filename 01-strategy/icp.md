# 🎯 Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary ICP: The Ambitious Gym Owner

### Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Who** | Independent gym owner, boutique studio founder |
| **Size** | 50–500 members, 1–3 locations |
| **Revenue** | €3k–€30k MRR |
| **Age** | 28–45 |
| **Markets** | Portugal, Spain, Brazil (Phase 1) → Europe-wide (Phase 2) |
| **Tech savvy** | Medium — uses WhatsApp daily, comfortable with apps |
| **Current software** | Mindbody, Glofox, Excel/WhatsApp, nothing |

### Their Top 3 Problems (ranked by pain)

1. **Member churn** — losing members after month 3 with no early warning
2. **Manual admin** — spending hours on scheduling, payments, WhatsApp messages
3. **No visibility** — don't know which classes are full, which members are at risk

### Their Dream Outcome

> "I want to walk into my gym and know the business is running itself. Members happy, payments collected, retention up — and I can focus on coaching."

### What Makes Them Buy

- Peer recommendation from a trusted gym owner
- Seeing a live demo with their real data
- Free migration from their current tool
- Price that doesn't feel like a punishment

### What Makes Them NOT Buy

- Complicated onboarding
- Long-term contracts
- Unclear pricing
- Doesn't support Portuguese payment methods (MB Way, Multibanco)

## Secondary ICP: The Fitness Instructor / PT

### Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Who** | Personal trainer, group class instructor, freelance PT |
| **Clients** | 10–50 active clients |
| **Revenue** | €2k–€8k/month |
| **Pain** | Scheduling chaos, no-shows, chasing payments, tracking client progress |
| **Current setup** | WhatsApp + Google Sheets + PayPal |

### Why They Matter

- They're inside the gyms we want to win
- They recommend tools to gym owners
- They refer their clients who become gym members
- They're the micro-influencers of the fitness world

---

## Tertiary ICP: The Gym Member

### Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Who** | Regular gym-goer, 3-5x/week |
| **Age** | 20–40 |
| **Goal** | Hit their fitness goals, stay consistent |
| **Pain** | Friction to book classes, no visibility of progress, no accountability |

### Why They Matter

- Happy members = gym owners stay subscribed
- Members on great apps leave reviews, tell friends
- Member NPS drives gym owner retention more than anything
