## Pricing Tactics

### Annual vs Monthly
- Annual discount: 20% (2 months free)
- Push annual first — reduces churn, improves LTV
- "Annual subscribers get priority onboarding and data migration for free"

### Early Adopter Deal (First 20 gyms)
- Lifetime 30% discount — locked in forever
- In exchange for: monthly feedback call + case study permission
- Creates urgency + loyalty + advocacy

### Free Trial
- 14-day free trial, no credit card required
- "Try it with your real members — if it doesn't blow you away, we won't charge you"

### Migration Offer
- Free data migration from Mindbody/Glofox
- 1 free month for migrating gyms
- Free parallel run (30 days both systems active)

---

## Psychological Pricing Notes

1. **Show annual savings prominently** — "Save €238/year vs monthly"
2. **Frame against competitor cost** — "vs €499/month on Mindbody"
3. **Show the ROI** — "Most gyms save €X/month just on payment processing fees"
4. **Don't price too low** — under €49/month signals "toy product" to gym owners
5. **Charge for the value of retention** — not just features. Retention = money.
