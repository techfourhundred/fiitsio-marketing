## Deep Dives

### Mindbody — The Giant Everyone Hates

**Revenue:** ~$200M ARR (ABC Fitness acquisition 2022, EGYM merger 2026)

**Why gyms use them:**
- Brand recognition, been around since 2001
- App marketplace (members discover gyms)
- Deep feature set

**Why gyms LEAVE them:**
- Merchant rates: 3.35% flat (even on debit!)
- 20% commission on new clients from their marketplace
- UI described as "dated and clunky"
- $699/mo for full features
- Support is terrible, tickets take weeks
- EGYM merger 2026 causing mass exodus

**Our angle:**
> "Mindbody charges you 3.35% on every transaction AND takes 20% of new clients they send you. We don't take a cut of your revenue. Ever."

**Messaging kill shot:**
> "Mindbody makes €15,000 a year from a gym doing €50k in revenue. Fiitsio makes you more money than we cost."

---

### Glofox — The Boutique Darling

**Revenue:** ~$20–30M ARR (ABC Fitness acquisition 2022)

**Why gyms use them:**
- Clean design
- Good class booking UX
- Decent branded app

**Why gyms LEAVE them:**
- Buggy member app
- Customer service inconsistent
- Pricing opaque (custom quotes, no transparency)
- Franchise-oriented, not great for independents
- Limited retention/churn analytics

**Our angle:**
> "Glofox looks great until your members can't book. Our app actually works."

---

### Wodify — The CrossFit King

**Revenue:** ~$15M ARR

**Strengths:** Great for CrossFit, workout tracking, WOD management

**Weaknesses:**
- Niche (CrossFit-specific culture)
- Add-on pricing is confusing
- Limited for traditional gyms

**Our angle:** Don't compete directly — position as "better for traditional gyms, yoga, and multi-sport facilities"

## Fiitsio's Unfair Advantages

### 1. Portuguese Market First
- EU/PT payment methods: MB Way, Multibanco, Easypay
- Portuguese UX (language, culture, tax requirements)
- Zero direct competitors with this focus
- Can dominate PT → expand to ES, BR from a position of strength

### 2. Modern Stack = Better UX
- React Native mobile app vs. competitors' webview apps
- Real-time everything (streaks, QR entry, notifications)
- Built 2024 — no 20-year-old tech debt

### 3. Retention Analytics Built-In
- Churn prediction (competitors charge extra for this)
- WhatsApp automation (no competitor does this natively)
- Achievement/gamification system

### 4. Founder-Told Story
- Kevin is building in public
- Direct line to a gym owner community
- Trust > brand for early adopters
